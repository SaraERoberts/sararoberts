About Me


My name is Sara Roberts. I'm currently a Business Consultant and Perficient serving as a Lead Program Manager for a Digital Media Software corporation, where I lead an onboarding program and drive engagements for enterprise-level customers (product migrations, deployments, troubleshooting, etc). Additional responsibilities include establishing annual KPIs, coaching a team, and reporting program metrics to client stakeholders. I am Scrum Master, Scrum Product Owner, and SAFe Agile certified and experienced in agile methodology, quality assurance and software development of web-based applications.

I received my first Bachelor of Science from Louisiana State University and my second Bachelor’s from Southeastern Louisiana University in Computer Science. As a student, I gained experience programming in Java, SQL, and JavaScript, and created several web applications in React/.NET. I also have several years’ experience as a manual QA Tester for EA Games. I leverage my technical background in my current role as a Business Consultant and Perficient.

I consider myself a genuine, creative, and collaborative team member and at work, I value independence, transparency, and honesty. I'm really passionate about solving complex problems alongside others and I am excited to grow my career in this direction.

I also mentor junior team members as a career guide, contribute as part of the Culture and Philanthropy pillar, write company blogs and sales memos, have won the Extra Mile award for several quarters, and many other extra curricular work activities. I have two calico cats and I enjoy watercoloring, arts and crafts, watching true crime, and karaoke.
